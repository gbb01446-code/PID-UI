HOW TO USE
  1. Select your preferred switches for each role below.
  2. Confirm the 3×3 grid preview looks correct.
  3. Click Download rfadj.lua and save the file.
  4. Copy rfadj.lua to your EdgeTX SD card: /SCRIPTS/FUNCTIONS/
